Melaka River Cruise

·         Information

The Melaka River Cruise is a great way to see the sights of Melaka at a leisurely pace, in comfort and without breaking the bank.

·         Operational Hours

Open daily = 9am until 11.30pm

·         Fees

My kad holders:

Adult = RM 25

Children = RM 15 (age 2-12)

Foreigners:

Adult = RM 30

Kids = RM 25

·         Location

Jalan Persisiran Bunga Raya, Kampung Bunga Paya Pantai, 75100, Melaka
